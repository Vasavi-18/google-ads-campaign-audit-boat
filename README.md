# Google Ads Campaign Audit — boAt Lifestyle

**Conducted by:** Vasavi Chinnala  
**Certification:** Google Ads Search Certified (2026) · Google Analytics Certified (2025)  
**Date:** June 2026  

---

## Overview

An independent audit of boAt Lifestyle's Google Search advertising strategy, 
conducted across 4 live keyword queries. The audit analyses ad copy, keyword 
coverage, and sitelink structure against Google Ads best practices.

---

## Methodology

- Live SERP observation across 4 search queries
- Google Keyword Planner research (12 keywords mapped by intent, volume, competition)
- Ad copy analysis against RSA best practices
- Sitelink evaluation against product-specific anchor standards

---

## Key Findings

| # | Finding | Priority |
|---|---------|----------|
| 1 | Zero paid presence on "wireless earphones under 2000" — highest-volume category keyword | 🔴 High |
| 2 | All Search ads tied to Payday Sale — no evergreen RSA variants | 🔴 High |
| 3 | Sitelinks use generic discount claims — no product-specific anchors | 🟡 Medium |

---

## Recommendations Summary

1. Add category keyword ad group targeting "wireless earphones under ₹2000" + 5 long-tail variants
2. Build RSA with 15 headline variants — 5 feature-led, 5 price-led, 5 USP-led
3. Replace sitelinks with product-specific anchors (name + price + key spec)
